WHO WE ARE

Based in Orlando, Florida, N.U.T.S Robotics—short for Nerds United for Technological Success—is the oldest of seven FIRST Tech Challenge teams at Orlando Science Middle and High School. Our team thrives on curiosity, creativity, and a passion for engineering, coding, and problem-solving. Composed of dedicated students, supportive mentors, and enthusiastic community members, we collaborate to design, build, and program advanced robots that compete at the highest level. At N.U.T.S, we aim not only to excel in robotics competitions but also to inspire a love for STEM in the next generation of innovators.
